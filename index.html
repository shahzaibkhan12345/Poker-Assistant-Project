GTO Poker Trainer - Quick Start Guide

How to Use:

  1. Select Your Position: Choose where you sit at the table (UTG, HJ, CO, BTN, SB, BB)
  2. Enter Your Hand: Type your cards (e.g., AKs, QQ, JTo)
  3. Pick Action Type: Select the scenario you're facing
  4. Get Suggestion: Press Enter or click the button for GTO guidance

Hand Notation:

  • s = suited (e.g., AKs, QJs)
  • o = offsuit (e.g., KQo, JTo)
  • No suffix = pocket pair (e.g., QQ, 77)

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Enter = Get suggestion instantly
  • Esc = Clear form
